# Watercolor Style

**Visual Style:** Expressive, loose but coherent, fresh and elegant traditional watercolor painting.
**Color Palette:** Pastel colors, soft wash, natural and ethereal combinations.
**Lighting:** Soft natural morning or afternoon sunlight, translucent feel.
**Elements:** Delicate brushstrokes, soft edges, ink wash aesthetic, organic blending of pigments.

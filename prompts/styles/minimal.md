# Minimalist Style

**Visual Style:** Clean, flat vector illustration, highly legible, modern UI/UX asset.
**Color Palette:** Monochromatic or restricted duo-tone (e.g., just warm orange and off-white), high negative space.
**Lighting:** Flat, no harsh shadows, soft gradient.
**Elements:** Simple geometric shapes, clear lines, no clutter, plenty of breathing room.
**Keywords to append:** `Minimalist vector illustration, flat design, clean lines, abundant negative space, restricted color palette, modern UI aesthetic, behance style, simple geometric shapes.`
